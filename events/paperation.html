PAPERATION

An idea can turn into dust or magic, depending on the talent that rubs against it. Come and present your paper at Asthra 2k21, Cuz ideas shouldn’t be kept to ourselves something must be done about them. .
RULES
1. Maximum number of participants in a team is 3.
2. Abstract of the paper of the respective topics must be sent along with the registration in google form before 25/04/2021.
3. Topics must be selected from the list of the topics given below,
• Artificial Intelligence
• Image Processing
• Cloud Computing
• Big Data Analytics
• Network Security
• Mobile Communication
• Internet of Things
• Future Trends of IT
Abstract and PPT should contain same topics. Not restricted to the above topics, new ideas will be welcomed.
4. The teams will get 15 mins for presentation including Q&A session.
5. Applicants must mention their complete contact details: name, College Name, College address, Email ID and phone number, along with the title of the paper.
6. Participants must switch on the video and audio while presenting.
7. PPT must contain minimum 6 slides.
8. Participants must ensure that they have strong internet connection.
9. Students from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, the same student may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
10. Papers will be judged on
Originality
Simplicity
Objectives
Methodology
Findings and Analysis
Conclusions
