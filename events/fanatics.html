FANATICS

Are you a filmophile or an otaku? If yes, here is a fun-filled super exciting event conducted by ASTHRA2k21 for those out there. Get your teammate and set aside because it’s time to play. In this event, we bring to you, a fun way to test your knowledge on these famous and beloved fandoms. Immerse into the world of “THE FANATICS” because everything else is child’s play.
This event comprises of totally 3 rounds. All three rounds are going to be conducted in Zoom meeting.


ROUND-1
This will be a Multiple-Choice Question Round with time restriction of [15 seconds per question]. A total of 20 – 25 questions will be streamed in the format of (mp4). Format of answer for FIRST ROUND: QUESTION NUMBER {space} OPTION
ROUND-2
Reasoning Question round (no options), where you will be shown videos or pictures and you will be asked questions related to it. There will be totally 20 – 25 question in this round, and it also has a time restriction of (20 – 25 seconds) based on the difficulty of the questions. Format of answer for SECOND ROUND: QUESTION NUMBER {space} ANSWER
ROUND-3
This will be a Buzzer round, where you will be asked Questions orally. Once the question is told we will notify you with “START”, you must press buzzer (i.e.) “HAND RAISE BUTTON”.
